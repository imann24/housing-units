PEPANNHU
 Annual Estimates of Housing Units for the United States, Regions, Divisions, States, and Counties:  April 1, 2010 to July 1, 2014

Note: The estimates are based on the 2010 Census and reflect changes to the April 1, 2010 housing
units due to the Count Question Resolution program and geographic program revisions. All
geographic boundaries for the 2014 housing unit estimates series are as of January 1, 2014.  For
the housing unit estimates methodology statement, see 
http://www.census.gov/popest/methodology/index.html.
